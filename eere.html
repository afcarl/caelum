EERE¶

Global EERE Weather Data Sources wrapper

Examples:

Annual GHI for STATION_CODE in Watts >>> sum([int(i[‘GHI (W/m^2)’]) for i in EPWdata(‘418830’)]) 1741512

Minimum Design Temperature >>> minimum(‘418830’) 8.8

2% Max Design Temperature >>> twopercent(‘418830’) 34.3
